Spontaneous Planner

There was once a site called FutureMessage.me. It was really cool.

You could send yourself (or anyone) an email in the future!

Specifically one to six months in the future! (at random)

I really wanted to use it to plan the spontaneous. That is, I would send myself a message like "Go rock climbing today", or "Bake pumpkin pie" with the understanding that, on the day I received the email I HAD to do it.

There's still FutureMe.org, but that one requires a specific date for receipt. (and that wouldn't be spontaneous , now would it?)

So I give you Spontaneous Planner! Reborn from the Ashes of FutureMessage.me!
